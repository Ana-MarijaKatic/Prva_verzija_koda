About us

The Study Cafe - Bright aims to take you one step closer to success. The key factors that define Study Cafe - Bright are comfortable seating, good lighting, a peaceful environment, cooperative staff, and a good internet connection.

You can choose between studying in a Quiet room or in a cafe environment among people who are chatting and drinking coffee.

Between study sessions in the Quiet room you can take a rest in our cafe area and purchase drinks or quick bites.

We believe that you will be able to increase your study hours and make yourself more productive seeing others studying or doing their work beside you.
